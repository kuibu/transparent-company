# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

[中文](#中文) | [English](#english)

---

## 中文

我们致力于构建一个专业、尊重、可协作的社区环境。

### 我们鼓励
- 尊重不同背景与观点
- 基于事实与证据进行技术讨论
- 在反馈中保持清晰、具体、建设性

### 不可接受行为
- 人身攻击、骚扰、歧视性言论
- 恶意挑衅、持续性破坏协作
- 泄露他人隐私或敏感信息

### 执行
维护者有权对不当行为采取警告、限制讨论、移除内容等措施。
如有问题，请通过 `/Users/a/repos/transparent-company/SUPPORT.md` 提供的渠道联系。

## English

We are committed to a professional, respectful, and collaborative community.

### Expected Behavior
- Respect people with different backgrounds and viewpoints
- Keep technical discussions evidence-based
- Provide feedback that is clear, specific, and constructive

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language
- Persistent bad-faith disruption
- Exposing private or sensitive information without consent

### Enforcement
Maintainers may issue warnings, restrict participation, or remove content when needed.
For concerns, use the channels listed in `/Users/a/repos/transparent-company/SUPPORT.md`.
